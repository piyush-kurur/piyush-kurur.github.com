About me

I work at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Kanpur. I try to make sense of the complex world around us through the tools of computer science: algorithms, complexity and logic. My research interests are centred around algorithms and complexity. Specifically, I am interested in computational algebra, quantum computation computational number theory. Recently, I have been interested in functional programming and type theory.
